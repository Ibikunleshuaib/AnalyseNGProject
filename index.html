How it works

Step 1: Tell us about your research/data

Write brief description of what your research is about in the form below or click on the whatsapp icon to chat with a data analyst.

Step 2: Forward your data

Attach softcopy of your research data in the form below or send via whatsapp. If your data is in hard copy form (e.g questionnaires), let us know in the message box in the form below and we will get in touch with you anywhere you are in Nigeria.

Step 3: Get results

Within a week or two of receipt of your data, our team of versatile data analysts would forward to you results of analysis carried out including detailed interpretation of results. After you've received the results, one of our data analysts would provide detailed explanation of the analysis carried out to enhance your understanding of interpreted outputs.

AnalyseNG

AnalyseNG is a full-fledged research and data analytics service provider. We help students, businesses and organizations define methodology of research projects, analyse research data using appropriate statistical tools and interpret results of data analysis in detailed and explanatory form. Our suite of services include data analysis, design and analysis of questionnaire, design and analysis of experiment, market research, business intelligence, academic research, training and consultancy services.
